Company

We're building the security layer every mobile app deserves — automated, intelligent, and invisible to users.

Mission

Make mobile app security effortless.

We believe every app — from a two-person startup to a Fortune 500 bank — deserves world-class runtime protection. Security shouldn't require a dedicated team of reverse engineers or months of integration work. It should be one step in your build pipeline.

Vision

A world where every app ships secure by default.

We envision a future where runtime protection is as standard as HTTPS — applied automatically, configured intelligently, and maintained without human intervention. Attackers lose their advantage the moment your app leaves the build server.

Our Story

Born from real-world frustration.

Before Armoryx, our founding team spent years as mobile security consultants for banks, fintech companies, and healthcare providers. We saw the same pattern everywhere: brilliant engineering teams shipping apps with zero runtime protection — not because they didn't care, but because existing solutions were too complex, too slow, and too expensive.

SDK-based tools required weeks of integration work. Manual obfuscation broke with every update. And compliance audits still flagged gaps. We built Armoryx to solve this — a platform that applies protection at the binary level, configures itself with AI, and fits into any CI/CD pipeline without touching a single line of source code.

Today, Armoryx protects thousands of apps across fintech, healthcare, gaming, and enterprise. Our team is distributed across San Francisco, London, and Singapore — united by the belief that security should be automated, not aspirational.

Values

What drives us

Security as a Right

Every app and every user deserves protection. We make enterprise-grade security accessible to teams of all sizes.

Zero Friction

If security creates friction, teams skip it. We obsess over making protection invisible to developers and users alike.

Transparency

We publish what we detect, how we respond, and where gaps exist. No security theater — only measurable protection.

Continuous Improvement

Attackers evolve daily. Our AI models and protection layers update continuously, staying ahead of emerging threats.

Philosophy

Security philosophy

We believe in defense in depth at the client level. No single protection technique is unbreakable — but layered, AI-coordinated defenses create an attack surface that's economically unviable to penetrate. Our approach combines obfuscation, integrity verification, environment detection, and behavioral analysis into a unified protection envelope.
