Features

A companion, not a chatbot.

Built around three things: memory that lasts, conversations that feel human, and a complete commitment to your privacy.

Long-term memory

Golpo remembers what you shared last week, last month, last year. Your companion grows alongside you.

Natural voice mode

Speak the way you’d speak to a friend. Realtime, low-latency voice with expressive intonation.

Always available

Developer SDK

Bring Golpo into your own apps.

One SDK, every platform. Add a companion to your product in a few lines, with streaming responses and persistent memory wired in by default.

  • Streaming responses with token-level deltas
  • Custom personas and tunable personality
  • Memory hooks — pull, write, redact
  • Realtime voice with sub-300ms latency
// Embed Golpo in your app
import { Golpo } from "@golpo/sdk";

const companion = new Golpo({
  persona: "warm-listener",
  memory: "persistent",
  voice: { enabled: true }
});

const stream = await companion.talk("How was your day?");

for await (const chunk of stream) {
  process.stdout.write(chunk.delta);
}
# Embed Golpo in your app
from golpo import Golpo

companion = Golpo(
    persona="warm-listener",
    memory="persistent",
    voice=True,
)

async for chunk in companion.talk("How was your day?"):
    print(chunk.delta, end="")
// Embed Golpo in your iOS app
import Golpo

let companion = Golpo(
    persona: "warm-listener",
    memory: .persistent,
    voice: true
)

for try await chunk in companion.talk("How was your day?") {
    print(chunk.delta, terminator: "")
}

FAQ

Things people ask first.

If your question isn’t here, reach out.

Is Golpo a therapist or mental health service?
No. Golpo is a companion — a thinking partner who listens, asks questions, and remembers. It is not a substitute for a licensed therapist, doctor, or crisis service. If you are in distress, please reach out to a qualified professional or crisis line.
Who can read my conversations?
Only you. Conversations are encrypted on your device before they are stored. Even our team cannot read your messages. You can export or permanently delete everything from Settings at any time.
What does Golpo remember, and can I edit it?
Golpo builds a private memory of facts you share — names, preferences, ongoing situations. You can review every memory, edit it, or remove it. Nothing is added without you seeing it.
Which languages does Golpo speak?
40+ languages including English, Bangla, Hindi, Urdu, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, and Arabic. You can also switch languages mid-conversation.
Is there a free tier?
Yes. The free plan includes unlimited text conversations and a 30-day rolling memory. Voice mode and persistent long-term memory are part of the paid plans.
Is Golpo open source?
Golpo Core — the local memory engine and SDK — is open source under Apache 2.0. The hosted product, voice models, and personality presets are proprietary.
