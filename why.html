Why Armoryx

Your backend is protected. Your infrastructure is locked down. But your mobile app runs on devices you don't control.

📱

Mobile Apps Need Runtime Security

Unlike web apps running on your servers, mobile apps execute on user devices — rooted phones, emulators, and attacker-controlled environments. Once your APK or IPA is published, it's in hostile territory. Static defenses aren't enough when the entire runtime is compromised.

🔓

Backend Security Isn't Enough

API authentication, WAFs, and server-side validation protect your infrastructure — not your client. Attackers reverse engineer your app to understand API logic, extract keys, bypass client-side checks, and manipulate requests before they ever reach your backend.

⚡

No-Code Protection Matters

Traditional mobile security requires SDK integration, months of engineering work, and ongoing maintenance. No-code protection applies directly to compiled binaries — meaning faster deployment, no developer bottleneck, and security that ships with every release automatically.

🤖

AI-Configured Defenses

Manual security configuration is error-prone and time-consuming. Armoryx's AI analyzes your app's attack surface, identifies vulnerabilities, and configures optimal protection — adapting as threats evolve without requiring human intervention.
